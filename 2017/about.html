--- layout: pdvw2017 title: About PDVW 2017 ---

The goal of the Pedagogy of Data Visualization Workshop is to create and sustain a community of educators interested in the pedagogy of data visualization. This interactive half-day workshop will be the beginning of a community of practice where participants will discuss their interests, challenges faced, and novel approaches that they have incorporated into their respective classrooms. See our Call for Participation for more information.
